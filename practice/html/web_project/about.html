Cliff Reilly is a 30 year old musician from San Francisco, CA. He began conducting at the age of 14 with the Peninsula Girls' Chorus and the Bay Bells handbell ensemble. He is constantly in demand from numerous groups around the Bay Area and received his Bachelor's in Music from Bucknell University in Lewisburg, Pennsylvania.
After five seasons with the San Francisco Symphony Chorus, Cliff founded two a cappella ensembles and directs one of them. For more information and a detailed CV please see the contact page.
